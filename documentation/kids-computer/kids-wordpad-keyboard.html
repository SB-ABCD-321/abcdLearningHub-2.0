WordPad — Your Writing Superpower! 📝

WordPad is a FREE program that comes with Windows! It's like a magical notebook on your computer where you can write stories, letters, and lists — and make them look BEAUTIFUL! ✨

How to Open WordPad 🖥️

1️⃣ Click the Start button (⊞) at the bottom left

2️⃣ Type 'WordPad' in the search box

3️⃣ Click on WordPad to open it!

⭐ Cool Things in WordPad!
𝐁

Bold Text

Make words thick and strong! Use Ctrl+B or click the B button!

𝑰

Italic Text

Make words lean to the side! Like they're running fast! Use Ctrl+I!

U

Underline

Draw a line under important words! Use Ctrl+U!

🎨

Font Color

Change text colors! Make rainbow colored writing!

📏

Font Size

Make letters BIG or small! Titles should be bigger!

💾

Save File

Save your work so you don't lose it! Press Ctrl+S always!

📖 Parts of WordPad Window

📍 Title Bar

Shows the name of your document at the top!

📍 Ribbon/Toolbar

Where all the formatting buttons are! Bold, Italic, etc.

📍 Writing Area

The big white space where you type your text!

📍 Status Bar

Shows information about your document at the bottom!

📍 Scroll Bar

On the right side — scroll up and down your document!

📍 Cursor

The blinking line that shows where you will type next!
