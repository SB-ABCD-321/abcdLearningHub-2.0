🖥️ Chapter 2

How to Open MS Paint

Great job learning the mouse! Now let's open MS Paint. There are 3 easy ways to do it!

1

🔍 Search for It (Easiest Way!)

Click the Search button (🔍) at the bottom of your screen. Type "Paint" and press Enter. Click on the Paint app when it appears!

2

🪟 Start Menu Way

Click the Windows button (⊞) at the very bottom left corner. Look for All Apps, then find Paint and click it!

3

⌨️ Keyboard Shortcut (For Experts!)

Hold ⊞ Win + press R, then type mspaint and press Enter. Whoosh — Paint opens like magic! ✨

⭐

Teacher Tip!

Once you've opened Paint before, you can find it quickly by right-clicking the taskbar icon and choosing "Pin to taskbar" — then it'll always be there at the bottom of your screen, ready to go!

🛠️ Chapter 4

Your Drawing Tools

These are your magical drawing tools! Each one does something different and special. Let's learn them all! 🧙‍♂️

✏️

Pencil

Draw thin, sharp lines. Move your mouse while holding the left button to draw freehand. Great for sketching!

Press P
🖌️

Brush

Like a real paintbrush! You can choose different sizes and styles. Makes softer, thicker lines.

Press B
⬜

Rectangle

Click and drag to draw perfect rectangles and squares. Hold Shift while dragging to make a perfect square!

Press R
⭕

Ellipse / Oval

Click and drag to draw ovals and circles. Hold Shift while dragging to make a perfect circle! 🔵

Press O
📏

Line

Draw perfectly straight lines! Click where you want to start, then drag to where you want to finish.

Press L
🪣

Fill with Color

Click inside any closed shape to fill it with color instantly! Like pouring paint into a bucket! 🪣

Press F
⬛

Eraser

Made a mistake? No problem! The eraser removes anything you've drawn. Just click and drag over it!

Press E
🔤

Text Tool

Add words to your picture! Click anywhere on the canvas, then type. You can change the font size too!

Press T
💉

Color Picker

Click on any color in your drawing to instantly use that exact color! Great for matching colors.

Press I
📐

Triangle / Polygon

Draw triangles and other shapes with straight sides. Click for each corner, double-click to finish!

Press G
🌟

Super Tip — Undo Mistakes!

Don't worry if you make a mistake! Press Ctrl + Z to undo — this magically erases your last action. You can press it many times to undo many mistakes! It's like a time machine! ⏰

🌈 Chapter 5

Colors & How to Use Them

Colors make your drawings come alive! Paint has lots of colors to choose from. Let's learn how to pick them!

🎨 Click a color to see it!

In real Paint, you'd click a color in the palette to start drawing with it. Try clicking below!

⚫

Black selected!

Now you can draw in this color! 🖌️

1

Color 1 (Your Drawing Color)

When you left-click a color in the palette, that becomes your main drawing color. This is what your pencil, brush, and shapes will use!

2

Color 2 (Your Background Color)

When you right-click a color, that becomes your background color. This is what the eraser uses when you erase!

3

Make Your Own Color!

Click "Edit colors" in Paint to mix your own unique colors! You can make any color you can imagine — even your favorite shade of blue! 💙

💾 Chapter 7

Saving Your Picture!

When you make a beautiful drawing, you'll want to save it so it doesn't disappear! Here's how:

1

🚀 Quick Save — Keyboard Shortcut!

Press Ctrl + S to save super fast! If it's your first time saving, a window will pop up asking you to give your picture a name.

2

📁 Save Using the Menu

Click File at the top left → then click Save As → Choose where to save it (like your Desktop or Pictures folder) → Type a name for your picture → Click Save!

3

📸 What File Type to Choose?

When saving, you'll see options like PNG, JPEG, BMP. For most drawings, choose PNG — it keeps your picture looking sharp and beautiful!

4

🖨️ Printing Your Drawing!

Want to print it on paper? Press Ctrl + P to print! Make sure a printer is connected. Then you can hang your art on the wall! 🏆

💡

Name Your Pictures Well!

Give your pictures helpful names like "My House Drawing" or "Blue Dragon" instead of just leaving it as "Untitled". That way you can find them easily later! 🔍

⭐ Chapter 8

Super Helpful Shortcuts!

These keyboard shortcuts will make you a Paint expert in no time! Practice them and impress your friends! 💪

↩️ Undo Mistake

Ctrl + Z

Made a mistake? This undoes it! Press multiple times to undo many steps!

💾 Save Picture

Ctrl + S

Save your work fast! Do this often so you don't lose your drawing!

🖨️ Print Drawing

Ctrl + P

Print your masterpiece on paper to show your friends and family!

🗂️ New Picture

Ctrl + N

Start a brand new blank drawing. Don't forget to save the old one first!

📋 Select All

Ctrl + A

Select your whole drawing at once! Then you can move or copy everything!

🔍 Zoom In

Ctrl + +

Make your drawing look bigger on screen for detail work! Use Ctrl - to zoom out!

✂️ Cut

Ctrl + X

Cut a selected piece to move it somewhere else!

📋 Paste

Ctrl + V

After copying or cutting, paste to put it in a new spot!

🎯

Perfect Shapes Trick!

When drawing a rectangle or oval, hold the Shift key while dragging. This makes PERFECT squares and circles every time! Magic! ✨
