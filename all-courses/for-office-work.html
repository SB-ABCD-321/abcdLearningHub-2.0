Full Programme Overview
This flagship diploma is the most demanded office course across West Bengal, consistently cited by local employers as a prerequisite for administrative and clerical roles. Covers all Microsoft Office applications at an advanced level — including Word (mail merge, styles, tables of content), Excel (conditional formatting, VLOOKUP, basic pivot tables), and PowerPoint (slide design, animations, presentations). Also includes desktop publishing (DTP) with PageMaker for creating newsletters, brochures, and official publications, plus CorelDraw basics for graphic elements in official documents.
