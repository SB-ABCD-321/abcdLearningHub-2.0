❓ Parent Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Q What age can my child start?
We have programmes for children as young as 5 years old. The ICGS programme is designed for ages 5–9, Smart Kids for 10+, and Kids Programming is open to all ages with no upper limit.
Q Does my child need their own computer?
No! ABCD Learning Hub provides all computers during class time. For practice at home, any basic computer or even a tablet works well — especially for Scratch coding.
Q How many children are in each class?
We keep class sizes very small — maximum 8–10 children per batch — so every child gets individual attention from the instructor throughout the class.
Q What certificate does my child receive?
Children receive a certificate from ABCD Learning Hub on completing each year or programme. These are recognised credentials that look great in school portfolios and future applications.
Q Are sibling discounts available?
Yes! We offer sibling discounts for families enrolling more than one child. Please WhatsApp or call us directly to discuss the available options for your family.
Q Can I attend a trial class first?
Absolutely — we offer a free trial class so your child can experience the learning environment before you commit. Contact us via WhatsApp to book your child's free trial session.
